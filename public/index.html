LEEJO HOTEL

Leejo Company proudly presents Leejo Hotel, a luxurious lodging situated in Central Jakarta. Our hotel, founded in 2022, offers top-notch amenities, facilities, and accommodations to ensure a one-of-a-kind experience for our guests. We embody excellence in every aspect of our hotel, from our cozy rooms to our personalized services. We cordially invite you to stay with us and indulge in the finest of Jakarta's offerings with style and comfort.

WORDIES SOCIAL MEDIA

Wordies Social Media, an innovative product by Leejo Company, is a text-only platform, similar to Twitter, that enables users to share their thoughts, feelings, and knowledge without visual distractions. Established in 2023, Wordies offers a unique, distraction-free space for written expression and global communication.

BERKALA COFEE

Berkala Coffee is a coffee shop created by Leejo Company, established in 2023 and located in Ampera, South Jakarta. Designed as a place for people to hang out and soak up the industrial-themed ambiance, Berkala Coffee offers a unique and inviting vibe for coffee lovers. Whether it's a quick cup to-go or a relaxing afternoon with friends, Berkala Coffee is the perfect destination to enjoy quality coffee and the company of others.
